⛳ Golf Habit
Phase 1 — Mobility + Strength Prompts
🔄 Flexibility (3–5 min)
Hip flexor stretch — kneeling
Kneeling lunge position, push hips forward gently. 30–45 sec each side. Unlocks trail hip loading in backswing.
90/90 hip stretch
Sit with both legs at 90°. Hold 30–45 sec per side. Builds the rotation range needed for hip/shoulder separation.
Thoracic rotation
Seated or standing — 10 slow reps each side. Upper back opening independently from hips. Trains the X-factor.
Wrist & forearm loosening
Circles, flexion/extension — 30 sec each. Keeps grip tension down through impact.
Reminder: Tick the Golf habit checkbox on the main page once done.
Phase 1 — Pick one, 5–10 min max
🎯 Putting (mat)
Free putting — 20 balls, any distance
No pressure. Just feel the stroke. This is Level 1 — showing up is the goal.
Gate drill (when ready)
Two tees just wider than your putter face. Roll the ball through without touching. Fixes stroke path.
Circle drill (Level 2 unlock)
5 balls around the hole at 1m. Get 5/10 consistently before moving up.
🏌️ Chipping (net)
Bump-and-run with 7-iron
10 chips. Low hands, quiet wrists. This is your highest-ROI scoring shot.
Wedge pitches (when ready)
Focus on clean contact and wrist hinge. 10 balls is enough.
